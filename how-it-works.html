Our Creative Financing Options

Seller Financing

In seller financing, the seller acts as the lender, allowing the buyer to pay them directly over time instead of securing a bank loan. It’s a flexible option for quick sales and buyers who can’t qualify traditionally.

Steps: Agree on terms, sign promissory note, make payments.

This method offers sellers steady income and buyers a path to ownership with fewer hurdles. It’s ideal for fast closings in Las Vegas.

Subject to Financing

With subject to financing, the buyer takes over the seller’s existing mortgage payments without a new loan. The mortgage stays in the seller’s name, but the property title transfers to the buyer.

Process: Negotiate, transfer title, take over payments.

It’s a fast solution for sellers in distress and buyers avoiding bank approvals, leveraging existing loan terms.

Lease Option

A lease option lets the buyer rent the property with the option to buy it later at a set price. Part of the rent may count toward the purchase, blending renting with ownership potential.

Flow: Lease, pay option fee, decide to buy or walk away.

Perfect for buyers needing time to prepare and sellers wanting income with a future sale locked in.
